# Vedashi Brand & Marketing Assets Guidelines

Welcome to the Vedashi Brand and Marketing Assets repository. This document outlines the core principles, visual identity, and marketing materials for the Vedashi Enterprise E-Commerce platform, a property of Frostrek AI.

## Brand Overview

**Vedashi** is a premier enterprise e-commerce and marketplace solution designed to empower vendors and provide an unparalleled shopping experience for consumers. Our brand stands for reliability, innovation, scalability, and elegance.

**Core Values:**
- **Trust & Reliability:** Ensuring secure and dependable transactions.
- **Innovation:** Cutting-edge technology for modern e-commerce.
- **Empowerment:** Giving vendors the tools they need to succeed.
- **Elegance:** A seamless and beautiful user experience.

## Visual Identity

### 1. Logo Guidelines
The Vedashi logo is our most recognizable asset. Please adhere to the following guidelines:
- **Primary Logo:** Always use the high-resolution primary logo for official communications.
- **Clear Space:** Maintain a minimum clear space around the logo equal to the height of the "V" in Vedashi to ensure visibility.
- **Minimum Size:** For digital use, the logo should never appear smaller than 30px in height. For print, no smaller than 0.5 inches.
- **Incorrect Usage:** Do not stretch, distort, or alter the colors of the logo. Do not place the logo on busy backgrounds that reduce legibility.

### 2. Color Palette
Our color palette reflects trust, modernity, and vibrancy.

- **Primary Colors:**

  - *Stellar White* (Clarity, Space)

## Brand Voice and Tone

Vedashi speaks with a voice that is:
- **Professional yet Approachable:** We are experts in our field, but we speak clearly and avoid unnecessary jargon.
- **Empowering:** We focus on what our platform enables our users to achieve.
- **Forward-thinking:** We emphasize innovation and future-proof solutions.

**Examples:**
- *Instead of:* "Our platform utilizes complex algorithms for vendor management."
- *Use:* "Vedashi simplifies vendor management, giving you the tools to grow your business effortlessly."

---

## Imagery and Photography

Our visual imagery should always reflect our core values.
- **People-Centric:** Show vendors and consumers actively engaging and succeeding.
- **High Quality:** Only use high-resolution, professionally lit images.
- **Authentic:** Avoid overly staged stock photos; lean towards authentic, relatable scenarios.
- **UI/Product Shots:** When showcasing the platform, use the latest, high-fidelity UI mockups.

## Marketing Materials

Within the `assets` directory, you will find organized folders containing:

1. **`/Logos`**: Vector (SVG, EPS) and raster (PNG, JPG) versions of the Vedashi logo.
2. **`/Social-Media`**: Templates and banners for LinkedIn, Twitter, and Instagram.
3. **`/Presentations`**: Pitch decks, product overviews, and slide templates.
4. **`/Print`**: Business cards, brochures, and event banners.
5. **`/Web-Banners`**: Promotional banners for campaigns and storefront highlights.
